Privacy Policy

We respect your family’s privacy. This summary explains what we collect and why.

Overview

SageDash provides tools to help parents communicate with teens. We collect only the data needed to deliver our service, improve it responsibly, and meet legal requirements.

Data we collect

  • Account data: email, password (hashed), subscription status.
  • Profiles (optional): parent/teen preferences to personalize lines.
  • Usage: pages visited, actions taken (e.g., “selected line”), device info.
  • Support: messages you send us.

How we use data

  • Provide and personalize the service (e.g., tailored lines).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Measure performance and improve features.
  • Send service emails (e.g., receipts, critical updates). You can opt out of non-essential emails.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share with processors that help us run the service (e.g., hosting, payments), under contracts that limit use to our instructions.

AI features

When enabled, text you submit may be processed by AI providers to generate responses. We use reputable vendors and restrict training on your content unless you opt in.

Retention

We keep data while you have an account and for a reasonable period after to comply with law, prevent abuse, and support accounting. You can request deletion at any time.

Children

SageDash is for parents/guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children.

Contact

Questions or requests: Support.
