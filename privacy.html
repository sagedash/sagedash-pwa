Privacy

We respect your privacy. SageDash provides educational guidance for parent–teen communication and does not offer medical or therapy advice.

Key points:

  • No provider training on your personal data.
  • Profile data is kept until you delete it.
  • Chat history is kept 12 months (pinned up to 36 months).
  • Referral and payout records kept indefinitely for lifetime payouts.
  • Billing documents kept 7 years.
