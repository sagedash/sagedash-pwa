Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

• Email for waitlist and account.
• Basic usage analytics (aggregated).
• Profile fields you provide to personalize guidance (e.g., tone, preferences).

How we use data

To deliver features, personalize guidance, improve the service, and communicate essential updates. We never sell your data.

Storage & retention

We keep data while your account is active or as needed for the service and legal obligations. You can request deletion at any time by emailing support@sagedash.app.

Security

We use reputable hosting and industry-standard safeguards. No method is 100% secure, but we strive to protect your information.

Children

Sagedash is for parents/guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll note the effective date and, when appropriate, notify you of significant changes.

Contact

Email support@sagedash.app for privacy questions or data requests.
