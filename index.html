FAQ

How are the “3 lines” written?

They follow a simple pattern: 1) Acknowledge + mirror, 2) Validate + encourage, 3) Choice + hope.

What’s the difference between Hint, Core, and Soul?

Hint uses curated lines by personality. Core generates tailored 3-line advice. Soul is free-style chat with monthly send limits.
