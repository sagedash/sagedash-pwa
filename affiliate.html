Affiliate Program

Share SageDash with other parents and earn recurring revenue as they subscribe.

Designed for counselors, parent groups, and anyone who loves helping families connect.

How It Works

Get your link

We give you a unique affiliate link that tracks the parents you refer.

Share in your flow

Add your link to emails, workshops, posts, or parent resources where it feels natural.

Earn as they subscribe

When a parent starts a paid plan through your link, you earn a share of their subscription.

Earnings & Percentages

  • Standard affiliates: Earn 20% recurring on each active paid subscription you refer.
  • SageDash Master members: Once you reach SageDash Master (1,000 Dashes + 30 used days), your rate increases to 40% on new referrals.
  • Lifetime mapping: Referred accounts stay connected to your affiliate ID for the life of their account.

Payouts

  • Schedule: Payouts are calculated monthly based on all active paid subscriptions you referred in the prior month.
  • Minimum payout: We issue payouts when your balance is at least $5 USD. Smaller balances roll forward to the next month.
  • Method: Payouts are made via our payment provider to the account details you provide in your affiliate settings.

Cancellations & Refunds

When parent cancels

You earn commission through the end of any paid period already billed. Future renewals do not generate commission.

Refunds

If a payment is refunded or marked as fraud, we may reverse the related commission on your next payout.

Account changes

If a parent changes plans, your percentage applies to their new active subscription amount.

Program Basics

  • Who can join: Individual parents, counselors, schools, PTAs, parenting groups, and other family-centered organizations.
  • Compliance: We ask affiliates to represent SageDash accurately as an educational parenting tool, not a medical or therapy service.
  • Program updates: Commission rates and terms may evolve over time; we’ll provide notice before making material changes.
