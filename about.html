About SageDash

We help parents turn tricky teen moments into short, warm words—fast.

Why we exist

Parents often know what they feel—but not exactly what to say. SageDash gives you three cozy lines you can say or text right now—always empathy first, never blame.

What “Sage” means

A calm, caring guide. Our coach—simply called Sage—mirrors your voice and your teen’s vibe so the words feel like you, tuned to them.

How it helps

Better words → better moments → better bond. We keep it short, huggable, and hopeful—so the conversation can start, not stall.
