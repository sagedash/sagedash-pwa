How it works

Step 1
Set quick profiles

Tell Sage how you parent and how your teen ticks—fast, friendly sliders.

Step 2
Pick the moment

Grades, friends, screens, sports. Choose a moment or describe your situation.

Step 3
Get 3 lines in seconds

Empathy first, validation, then choice/hope—ready to say or send.

Sample lines

Tough grade today
  • That stings—come here a sec?
  • You tried. We’ll regroup together.
  • Hug or quiet first—your call.
Friend drama
  • Oof, that sounds heavy.
  • Your heart is so good.
  • Want ears or advice?
Screen time balance
  • Let’s make a tiny rule.
  • We’ll test it one week.
  • Ready to pick the start?
