How It Works

Set a quick profile, pick the moment, get three short lines in seconds.

Step 1

Set quick profiles

Tell Sage a bit about you and your teen so the lines sound like you—tuned to them.

Step 2

Pick the moment

Bad grade, friend drama, screen time standoff—choose what’s happening right now.

Step 3

Get 3 lines in seconds

Use as-is or tweak. Empathy first, no blame, a hopeful next step—every time.

Sample lines

Tough grade today

  • Oh, that stings. I know you worked hard.
  • One test doesn’t define you—your effort does.
  • Want a hug, a walk, or quiet time?

Friend drama

  • That sounds heavy. I’m really sorry, love.
  • Your feelings make sense—this stuff is hard.
  • Want to vent, plan, or just chill?

Screen time balance

  • I get it—screens are tempting, especially today.
  • Let’s find a rhythm that helps you feel good.
  • Want to set a timer or try a swap?
